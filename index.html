About

There are two reasons why I am passionate about software development. First, as a business consultant, I realized the critical role that technology plays within a business. During my downtime, I began to explore different areas in the technology industry. One of which was coding. As I began to code, I realized the many possibilities that there is in this field. Secondly, in music or coding, my passion is to create. To share and solve things that are yet to be solved for the world to enjoy. As a result of this, I am passionate about developing software. I desire to see businesses grow and succeed in a competitive market by creating excellent and seamless user experience software.

As a Software Developer, I am interested in:

  • Creating software with excellent functionality and seamless user experience.
  • Working alongside developers who have the same objective and mission statement.
  • Working with a team of developers to solve things that are yet to be solved.
  • Working with Senior developers to become a better developer with them.
  • Having fun coding with my teammates!

Outside of coding, I keep myself active by either working on my family's farm, bike-riding or hiking in different trails. Suppose I am not spending time outdoors. Then I am either listening to various podcasts/music on Spotify or playing the piano. If I'm not doing any of those things, I spend quality time with my wife and friends, finding the best coffee shop or Brazilian steakhouse around!
